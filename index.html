--- layout: page description: "A Powerful Platform for Robotics Research and Teaching" ---

The MIT RACECAR is an open-source powerful platform for robotics research and education.

The platform houses state-of-the-art sensors and computing hardware, placed on top of a powerful 1/10-scale mini race car.

The design and development of the RACECAR is a joint effort between the BeaverWorks Initiative of the Lincoln Laboratory, the Department of Aeronautics & Astronautics, and the Laboratory for Information and Decision Systems at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

All material is open source and readily available on GitHub.
